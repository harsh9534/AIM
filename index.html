Placements at AIM

View all

We have a good track record for achieving good placements every year. All eligible students with acceptable CGPA are provided with opportunities to secure a placement in FMCG, Consumer Durables, BFSI and other sectors. Average placement over the last couple of years has been 90%. Few students who opted out of placement to pursue their own venture areprovided with necessary support from the institute for nurturing their visionto convert to reality. Several of global prestigious companies in private sector are regularrecruiters of our students.
